The first step is the creation of a **workspace**. 

A workspace is the **representation of your organisation**. In other words, this can be ***your name***, or ***your company name***.

:::tip example
We have a pizzeria called "**John's Pizza**" - this is our business, so that's what we will call our workspace.
:::
